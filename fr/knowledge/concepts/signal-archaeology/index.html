CONCEPT

Archéologie du signal

Signal archaeology est le interpretation de technical, sonic, visuel ou archival traces comme evidence de hidden processes, transformations et histories.

Signal archaeology gives Electronic Artefacts a way à connect audio analysis, médias residue, archival provenance, mémoire et culturel interpretation.

active observed v1.0.0

Editorial claims

  2. Machine listening can support interpretation but cannot replace provenance.

Boundaries

What this concept excludes

  1. Nostalgic aesthetics avec no evidential méthode
