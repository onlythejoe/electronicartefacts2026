CONCEPT

Intelligence augmentée

Augmented intelligence est le design de outils, interfaces et systèmes that expand human perception, reasoning, mémoire et creative capability.

Augmented intelligence reframes computation comme a partner dans human work, linking HCI, AI, systèmes de connaissance, creative outils et Electronic Artefacts runtime recherche.

Definition scope

What this concept includes

  1. Human-computer interaction
  2. Creative outils
  3. AI assistance
  4. Knowledge work
  5. Interface design

Editorial claims

Position maintained by Electronic Artefacts

  1. Augmentation est un better frame than replacement pour many creative et recherche systèmes.
  2. Good augmented systèmes preserve context, reversibility et user judgment.

Boundaries

What this concept excludes

  1. Claims that autonomous systèmes replace human judgment dans all contexts
  2. Productivity automation avec no support pour understanding ou agency
