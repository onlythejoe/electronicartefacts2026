CONCEPT

Motion design

Motion design est le use de time, movement, transition, rhythm et sequencing à structure visuel communication et interface behavior.

Motion design relie visuel culture, interface sémantiques, typographey, animation, sound, choreographey et web interaction.

active validated v1.0.0

Definition scope

What this concept includes

  1. Animation
  2. Time
  3. Interface transitions
  4. Rhythm
  5. Visuel communication

Editorial claims

Position maintained by Electronic Artefacts

  1. Motion design can communicate state, causality, continuity et attention.
  2. Motion should be treated comme interface sémantiques, not only decoration.

Boundaries

What this concept excludes

  1. Decorative movement that does not clarify state, hierarchy ou meaning
  2. Animation that blocks access à content ou control
