CONCEPT

Provenance

Provenance is information about the people, activities, sources, transformations et decisions involved dans producing a thing ou piece de données.

Provenance helps readers assess reliability, rights, authorship, lineage, context et trust across recherche, archive, software et culturel records.

active validated v1.0.0

Editorial claims

Position maintained by Electronic Artefacts

  1. Provenance is essential pour culturel archives et AI-readable systèmes de connaissance.
  2. Provenance should distinguish source, author, curator, publisher et maintainer roles.

Boundaries

What this concept excludes

  1. A credit line avec no explanation de source ou transformation
  2. A timestamp without relation à production ou custody
