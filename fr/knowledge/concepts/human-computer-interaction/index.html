CONCEPT

Human Computer Interaction

Human computer interaction est le study et design de relationships between people, computational systèmes, interfaces, tasks, environments et cultures de use.

HCI relie interface design, accessibility, creative outils, augmented intelligence, software ergonomics et the social consequences de computation.

Editorial claims

Position maintained by Electronic Artefacts

  1. HCI is central à creative outils because interfaces shape what creators can perceive et attempt.
  2. Good interfaces expose state, preserve agency et make recovery possible.

Boundaries

What this concept excludes

  1. Pure visuel styling avec no relation à interaction, cognition ou use
  2. Automation design that ignores human agency et error recovery
