CONCEPT

Modélisation de graphe

Graphe modèleing represents a domain through addressable nodes, typed relationships et properties chosen à answer specific questions et support specific operations.

Graphe modèleing makes many-à-many relationships, paths, provenance et evolving context explicit where folders et rigid trees obscure them.

Editorial claims

Position maintained by Electronic Artefacts

  1. Graphes sont strongest when relationships sont first-class et many valid paths must coexist.
  2. Trees et folders remain useful projections even when the underlying modèle est un graphe.

Boundaries

What this concept excludes

  1. Turning every field into a node without a query ou reuse requirement
  2. Assuming a graphe donnéesbase automatically produces a good domain modèle
