## Thesis

Voice Capture Studio is not only a product page. It is a compact knowledge neighborhood around trustworthy audio interaction, local processing, structured evidence, open exports and browser capability boundaries.

## Selection logic

The collection includes the live project, the repository artefact and a small vocabulary of concepts that make the workflow understandable: speech recording, speech datasets, browser software, voice technology, machine-learning workflows, open source, web audio, metadata, provenance and HCI.

## Editorial role

Use this collection when the question is not simply “what is the app?” but “what kind of recording workflow does this app represent?”

## Current members

The collection starts with the project and repository, then connects them to durable concepts, technologies and six complete articles. These publications create routes from product evidence toward audio UX, provenance, deterministic research, lexical segmentation, mode design and browser-native architecture.

## Future expansion

Future members should include release notes, export examples and dataset-readiness reports once those artefacts can be published without exposing private voice data.
