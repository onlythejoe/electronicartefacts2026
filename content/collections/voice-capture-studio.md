## Thesis

Voice Capture Studio is not only a product page. It is a compact knowledge neighborhood around local speech recording, structured exports and browser-based privacy boundaries.

## Selection logic

The collection includes the live project, the repository artefact and a small vocabulary of concepts that make the workflow understandable: speech recording, speech datasets, browser software, voice technology, machine-learning workflows, open source, web audio, metadata, provenance and HCI.

## Editorial role

Use this collection when the question is not simply “what is the app?” but “what kind of recording workflow does this app represent?”

## Current members

The collection starts with the project and repository, then connects them to the durable concepts and technology records that explain their role in the Electronic Artefacts graph.

## Future expansion

Future members should include release notes, screenshots, export examples and dataset-readiness reports once those artefacts can be published without exposing private voice data.
