## Role

The transformer processes token relationships through attention and feed-forward layers, enabling parallel training and flexible sequence modeling.

## Use

Electronic Artefacts uses the transformer as a technical reference for explaining LLMs and multimodal systems rather than as a synonym for intelligence.
