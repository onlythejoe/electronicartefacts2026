PRIVACY

How the site handles data.

Electronic Artefacts keeps the site lightweight: there is no analytics stack, no advertising tracker and no third-party marketing pixel on the website.

Contact data

Email only when you write

If you contact the studio by email or through an external platform, the information you send is used only to answer your request and manage the conversation.

Local preferences

Stored in your browser

The site remembers a couple of interface preferences in localStorage, such as compact view and vivid mode. These settings stay on your device and are not shared with third parties.

External services

Third-party platforms

Instagram, GitHub, SoundCloud and VASTE are external services with their own privacy rules. Opening them leaves the Electronic Artefacts site.

Technical providers

GitHub Pages and IONOS

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages and its domain name is managed through IONOS. These providers may process technical connection data according to their own terms.

Retention

Kept only as needed

Email conversations are retained only for the time needed to answer and follow up on the request, then archived or removed according to the studio workflow.

COOKIES AND TRACKERS

No tracking cookies on this site

The current site does not include analytics cookies or advertising trackers. If those tools are added later, this policy will be updated accordingly.

The only browser storage used by the site is localStorage for interface preferences. That storage is functional and local to the device.
